Details

Teaser: Historic 16th-century defensive tower in Monterosso's old town
History: Built as part of the Genoese Republic's coastal defense network to protect against Saracen raids

Description

A 16th-century defensive tower that once served as part of Monterosso's coastal fortification system. Now housing shops and offering panoramic views from its base, it represents the village's maritime defense heritage.
