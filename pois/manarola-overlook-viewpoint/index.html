Description Long

This is arguably the most iconic and Instagrammable viewpoint in all of Cinque Terre. The overlook sits on a small promontory above Manarola, offering unobstructed views of the village's famous pastel-colored houses stacked on the cliff, the tiny harbor below, and vineyards climbing the hills behind. The viewpoint is particularly magical at sunset when the buildings glow in golden light. The area includes a small park with benches and shaded areas. Nearby is the famous Nessun Dorma restaurant which shares similar views. This is the classic Cinque Terre postcard view that appears in countless photos and travel magazines.

Description Short: The most famous and photographed viewpoint in Cinque Terre, offering iconic views of Manarola's colorful houses cascading down to the harbor.
Historical Significance: Traditional Mediterranean coastal settlement dating back to Roman times
