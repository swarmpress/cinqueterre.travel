History

Run by a Vernazza local who gained mixologist credentials in the United States, this establishment combines international expertise with deep knowledge of local wine traditions and Ligurian culinary heritage.

Highlights: Expert wine tastings, pesto making classes, traditional focaccia, Ligurian oil tastings, intimate cave setting

Description

This intimate underground wine cellar offers exceptional guided tastings of local Cinque Terre wines paired with traditional Ligurian specialties, plus hands-on pesto making classes in an authentic cave-like setting.
