Details

Teaser: Vernazza's picturesque main street with traditional shops, restaurants, and vineyard views
History: Originally built over an ancient underground river, Via Roma has been the commercial and social heart of Vernazza for centuries, connecting the village's upper reaches to its vital harbor below.
Highlights: Traditional shops, local restaurants, gelaterias, vineyard views, historic architecture, pedestrian-friendly

Description

Via Roma is Vernazza's charming main street that winds through the village from the train station to the harbor, lined with traditional shops, gelaterias, restaurants, and offering glimpses of the spectacular terraced vineyards above.

Architecture: Traditional Ligurian narrow street design with colorful houses, stone archways, and medieval layout
