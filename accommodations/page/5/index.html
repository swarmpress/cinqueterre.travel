B&B Da Baranin

In the historic center of Manarola, offers rooms, suites with breakfast and an apartment with kitchen use. Gluten free options available. Some products served come from bio-organic farming and fair trade.

Casa Di Mezzo by Arbaspàa

One of the best apartments in Manarola. The company manages several houses in different locations throughout the town, all accommodating up to four or five people. Offers sell out quickly so book well in advance.

Hotel Marina Piccola

Recently refurbished small hotel with a contemporary feel and modern amenities. Has 12 rooms (7 doubles, 4 triples and a Junior Suite) that are quiet and recently renovated. The star feature is its view across to Manarola's marina, with exposed stone, stylish furnishings, and modern comforts. Includes excellent Ligurian seafood restaurant and prime spot for beach and coastal path. Easy to reach from train station (5 minutes walk).

La Linea d'Acqua

Centrally located guesthouse offering peaceful stay, one-bedroom with modern amenities including air conditioning and Wi-Fi.
