Manarola to Riomaggiore via Beccara

Name: Manarola to Riomaggiore via Beccara
Rank: 10

Tips

Rest Stops: Ridge area has natural resting spots with panoramic views, stone walls for seating
Photo Spots: Ridge viewpoint for both villages, Montenero tip eastward, Cinque Terre coastline westward
Insider Tips: Free alternative to Via dell'Amore when it's closed or crowded. The trail offers best views of the Christmas nativity scene from above.

Stats

Distance Km: 1.5
Duration Hours: 1

Safety

Warnings: Steep terrain with 620 steps on each side, can be slippery when wet, no shade on ridge section

  • Starting station only - carry sufficient water

Details

Teaser: Historic mountain route with breathtaking ridge views between two coastal villages
Scenery: Ridge panoramas, cultivated terraces, coastal villages, Mediterranean landscapes
Terrain: Steep ascent, ridge walking, ancient stone steps descent, vegetable gardens
Highlights: Costa Corniolo ridge, panoramic coastal views, ancient stone steps, traditional terraced landscapes

Description

The original connection between Manarola and Riomaggiore, this challenging trail climbs over Costa Corniolo ridge offering spectacular panoramic views before descending via ancient stone steps to Riomaggiore.
