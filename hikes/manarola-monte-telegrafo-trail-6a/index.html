Manarola to Monte Telegrafo via Trail 6a

Name: Manarola to Monte Telegrafo via Trail 6a
Rank: 11

Tips

Rest Stops: Several scenic clearings along ascent, summit area with natural seating, panoramic rest spots
Photo Spots: Summit panorama with entire Cinque Terre coastline visible, mountain ridge compositions, sunrise/sunset colors
Insider Tips: Start very early (6 AM) to avoid afternoon heat and weather changes. The summit offers views from Portovenere to Levanto and inland mountains.

Stats

Distance Km: 8
Duration Hours: 4
Highest Point M: 516
Elevation Gain M: 600
Elevation Loss M: 600

Safety

Warnings: Challenging mountain terrain, weather can change rapidly, inform someone of your route, carry emergency supplies

Water Points

  • Manarola village only - carry sufficient water

112Mountain Rescue: 118

Details

Teaser: Summit the highest peak above Cinque Terre for ultimate panoramic views
Scenery: Panoramic coastal views, mountain ridges, forest canopy, alpine meadows
Terrain: Steep mountain paths, rocky sections, forest trails, summit approaches
Highlights: Monte Telegrafo summit (516m), 360-degree panoramic views, mountain wilderness, connection to Alta Via trail system

Description

A demanding high-altitude trail leading to Monte Telegrafo, the highest peak overlooking Cinque Terre, offering spectacular 360-degree panoramic views of the entire coastline and surrounding mountain ranges.
