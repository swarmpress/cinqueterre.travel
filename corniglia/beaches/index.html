The Art of Clifftop Swimming

Corniglia's unique position as the only village not directly on the sea creates an extraordinary coastal experience. The dramatic elevation provides breathtaking panoramic views while creating natural platforms for cliff jumping and secluded swimming.

The village's Roman agricultural terraces cascade down toward hidden rocky shores, where crystal-clear Mediterranean waters meet ancient stone walls. These secluded spots offer some of the most pristine swimming in all of Cinque Terre, accessible only to those willing to navigate steep coastal paths.

Timing is everything here. Early morning visits reward you with golden light illuminating the terraced hillsides, while afternoon swims offer the warmest water temperatures. The absence of sandy beaches means fewer crowds but requires proper footwear and swimming confidence.
