Sagra dell'Acciuga Fritta (Fried Anchovy Festival)

Name: Sagra dell'Acciuga Fritta (Fried Anchovy Festival)

Details

History: Anchovy fishing was Monterosso's main activity with a large cooperative of fishermen active through the 1980s. The festival honors this tradition and the PAT-designated Monterosso anchovies.

Organizer

Name: Pro Loco Monterosso and Comune di Monterosso
Type: Municipality
Website: http://www.prolocomonterosso.it
Contact Email: infoprolocomonterosso@gmail.com
Significance: Celebrates Monterosso's maritime heritage and its famous anchovies, once the village's primary economic activity
Official Event: Yes

Description Long

This gastronomic festival celebrates the famous Monterosso anchovies, a PAT (Traditional Agricultural Product) designation. From early morning, anchovies are fried in the characteristic iron fish-shaped pans and served with sgabei (local fried bread) and Cinque Terre white wine. The festival fills Piazza Garibaldi and the village streets with food stalls, artisan market vendors, and the irresistible aroma of freshly fried fish. June and July are the best months for catching anchovies, making this the perfect time to enjoy them fresh. The event includes live music, cultural activities, and celebrates the maritime traditions that have sustained this fishing village for centuries.

Tradition Origin: Historical
Description Short: A celebration of Monterosso's famous anchovies, freshly fried in traditional fish-shaped iron pans and served with local wine and sgabei.
