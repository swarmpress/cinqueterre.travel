Sagra dell'Acciuga Salata (Salted Anchovy Festival)

Name: Sagra dell'Acciuga Salata (Salted Anchovy Festival)

Details

History

Traditional anchovy salting in chestnut barrels has been practiced for centuries in Monterosso. The 40-60 day curing process in the dark creates the distinctive pink-brown color and concentrated flavor.

Organizer

Name: Pro Loco Monterosso
Type: Non-Profit
Significance: Celebrates the traditional preservation method that allowed fishing communities to store their catch before refrigeration
Official Event: Yes

Description Long

This September festival celebrates the traditional art of anchovy preservation. After 40-60 days of curing in salt-filled chestnut barrels, the anchovies are ready to be enjoyed. The salting process is a traditional method that has preserved this delicacy for generations. Festival-goers can taste salted anchovies prepared in various ways - with oil, oregano, and garlic, or simply with warm bread and Cinque Terre wine. Food stalls offer anchovy-based dishes, cooking demonstrations show traditional preparation methods, and the event celebrates the maritime culture of Monterosso.

Tradition Origin: Historical
Description Short: Celebration of traditionally preserved salted anchovies with tastings, demonstrations, and local products after the curing season.
