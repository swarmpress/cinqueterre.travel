Sciacchetrail - Cinque Terre Ultra Trail

Name: Sciacchetrail - Cinque Terre Ultra Trail

Details

History: Created to revive agricultural soul of territory and transfer tourism resources to agriculture sector. The name combines Sciacchetrà wine with trail running.

Organizer

Name: Polisportiva Cinque Terre
Type: Non-Profit
Website: https://www.sciacchetrail.com
Significance: Celebrates the connection between wine cultivation, landscape preservation, and athletic challenge. Promotes sustainable tourism and honors traditional viticulture.
Official Event: Yes

Description Long

Sciacchetrail unites trail running with love for the Cinque Terre territory. Named after Sciacchetrà, the precious sweet passito wine produced from terraced vineyards, the race follows ancient paths along dramatic cliffs and through villages. The 47km route has 2,600m elevation gain, while the 100km ultra extends into Val di Vara with 5,000m elevation. This event celebrates the hard work of generations of winemakers who shaped this UNESCO landscape, combining athletic challenge with cultural appreciation.

Tradition Origin: Modern
Year Established: 2016
Description Short: A challenging trail running race through terraced vineyards and coastal paths of Cinque Terre, celebrating the connection between wine culture and the landscape.
