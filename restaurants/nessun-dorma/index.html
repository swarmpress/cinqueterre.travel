Tips

  • Download the official app and get in the virtual queue starting at 3:45pm for the best sunset spots
  • Arrive early in the day (12-2pm) for lunch to avoid the evening crowds
  • Try the local anchovies - they're a Cinque Terre specialty and incredibly fresh
  • Bring a light jacket as it can get windy on the clifftop terrace in the evening
  • Perfect spot for photos - the golden hour lighting is spectacular for Instagram shots
